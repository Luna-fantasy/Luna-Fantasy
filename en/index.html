Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Luna.

What is Luna?

Luna is a massive, integrated fantasy world built on Discord. It features over 200 unique characters, rich lore spanning centuries, and a unique strategic card game called Luna Fantasy.

How do I join the community?

Simply click the "Join Discord" button to access our Discord server. Once there, you can create your character and start your journey in the Luna universe.

Is Luna free to play?

Yes! Luna is completely free to join and play. Simply join our Discord community and start your adventure.

How does the card game work?

Each player holds 5 cards of different rarities (Common, Rare, Epic, Unique + 1 Random). The game is about strategy and predicting your opponent's moves. Higher tier doesn't always guarantee victory!

Can I create my own character?

Absolutely! Our community encourages creativity. You can create original characters that fit within the Luna universe and participate in ongoing storylines.

What languages are supported?

Luna supports both Arabic and English. Our website and community welcome players from all backgrounds.
