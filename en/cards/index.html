What is Luna Fantasy?

Luna Fantasy is the official card game of the Luna universe. Created for the Luna Discord roleplay community, it combines strategy with the rich lore of our world.

How to Play?

Luna Fantasy is a unique card game invented by us. Each player holds 5 cards. You must predict your opponent's moves. It's about strategy, not luck.

  • Each player holds 5 cards (Common, Rare, Epic, Unique + 1 Random).
  • The winner is usually the stronger card, but higher tier doesn't always guarantee a win blindly.
  • A player must win 5 rounds to be the victor.
  • You can use the Random Card as a substitute for any of your existing cards.
