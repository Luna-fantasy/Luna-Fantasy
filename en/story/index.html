The Chronicles

The official lore, timeline, and the beginning of the Great Chaos War.

Chapter One

The Age of Peace

Long ago, the crescent island of Luna stood as a beacon of hope and prosperity. Its knights were legendary, its mages unmatched, and its people lived in harmony under the watchful gaze of the Council of Elders.

For centuries, Luna flourished. Trade routes connected the island to distant lands, bringing wealth and knowledge. The Academy of Mystic Arts trained generations of powerful sorcerers, while the Order of the Crescent Shield defended the realm from any who would threaten its peace.

"In those golden days, we believed nothing could break us. We were wrong."

But darkness was stirring in the shadows. In the forgotten depths beneath the island, an ancient power was awakening - one that would change the fate of Luna forever.

Chapter Two

The Chaos War

The arrival of Movarth, the King of Chaos, marked the end of Luna's golden age. With an army of corrupted souls and dark creatures, he swept across the island like a plague.

One by one, the great fortresses fell. The Order of the Crescent Shield fought valiantly, but they were no match for the overwhelming darkness. Within months, nearly half the island was consumed by chaos.

"When the King of Chaos smiled, even the bravest knights trembled."

All seemed lost. The Council of Elders had fallen, the Academy was in ruins, and hope was fading. But in the darkest hour, two unlikely heroes would emerge to turn the tide of war.

Chapter Three

The Two Masterminds

From the ashes of defeat rose Buddha Ban and the mysterious figure known only as The Mastermind. Where others saw only despair, they saw opportunity.

Through cunning strategy, forbidden alliances, and sacrifices that would haunt them for generations, they orchestrated a counter-offensive that would become legend. The Battle of the Crescent Moon lasted for three days and three nights.

"Victory was not won by strength alone, but by those willing to play the longest game."

When the dust settled, Movarth was sealed away in the depths from which he came. But the cost was immense. Luna would never be the same, and the two masterminds disappeared into legend, their true identities forever shrouded in mystery.
